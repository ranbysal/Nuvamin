Check the packaging and vials on delivery. Lyophilised peptides tolerate transit well; store as directed on each product page (typically −20 °C, desiccated, protected from light) as soon as the box arrives.

Returns

Temperature-sensitive research materials cannot be restocked once dispatched, so we do not accept change-of-mind returns. We refund or replace in full any order that arrives damaged, incorrect, or out of specification against its certificate of analysis.
