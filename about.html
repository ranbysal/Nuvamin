The company

We started Nuvamin
because the label
should be enough.

The supplement industry runs on ambiguity: proprietary blends, borrowed science, doses too small to do anything but appear on a label. Nuvamin is an argument that it can be done differently — slower, plainer, and in public.

Our standards

Six rules,
zero exceptions

(01)

Evidence first

An ingredient earns its place with published human data — not a rodent study, not a press release. When the evidence is thin, we say so on the label.

(02)

Clinical doses

We match the doses used in the research we cite, or we leave the ingredient out entirely. No fairy-dusting. No “proprietary blend” totals hiding the arithmetic.

(03)

Bioavailable forms

Glycinates over oxides, methylated B-vitamins, rTG omega-3, MK-7. The forms cost more. They are also the ones your body was expecting.

(04)

Independent verification

Every production batch goes to an ISO 17025 accredited laboratory that we do not own, fund, or influence. Certificates ship in the box and live online for five years.

(05)

Radical label honesty

Every excipient is listed, including the boring ones. If a capsule contains rice flour to fill space, the label says rice flour. Nothing hides behind “natural flavourings”.

(06)

Honest expectations

Supplements close gaps; they do not perform miracles. We would rather undersell and keep you for a decade than overpromise and lose you in a month.

The process

From paper
to capsule

Each formula starts as a literature review and ends as a certificate. In between: sourcing against a written specification, small-batch encapsulation in EU GMP facilities, quarantine until independent results return, and release only when every number lands inside spec. It takes longer. That’s fine — we are not in a hurry.

Step 1

Review

A formula begins with the question: what does the human evidence actually support, and at what dose?

Step 2

Source

Raw materials are bought against a written spec — origin, form, purity — from suppliers who accept audits.

Step 3

Make

Small batches, EU GMP facilities, no dyes or unnecessary excipients. Every run is quarantined on completion.

Step 4

Prove

An independent lab confirms identity, potency and purity. Only then does a batch get a certificate — and a customer.
