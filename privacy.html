Privacy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Who we are

Nuvamin ("we", "us") operates this website and supplies research-use-only compounds to qualified researchers and institutions. The data controller is Nuvamin. You can reach us at support@nuvamin.bio.

2. What we collect

  • Order details — your name, email address, shipping address, the items you order, and order totals.
  • Payment confirmation — a transaction reference and, at most, the last four digits of the card used. Card details are entered only on our payment provider’s PCI-compliant hosted page; we never see, transmit or store full card numbers.
  • Correspondence — messages you send us by email or the contact form.
  • Verification records — your acknowledgement of the age and researcher-eligibility requirements, stored in your browser.
  • Technical information — basic server logs such as IP address, browser type, device information and access times, plus cart data kept in your browser’s local storage.

3. Why we process it

We use personal data to review and process orders, take payment, arrange delivery, send receipts and certificates of analysis, respond to support requests, maintain business records, comply with accounting and tax obligations, secure the website and prevent fraud. Depending on the activity, we rely on performance of a contract, legal obligations, our legitimate interests in operating and protecting the business, or consent where required. Nuvamin operates under the applicable laws of Ohio and the United States of America; additional privacy laws may apply based on where you live.

4. Who receives it

Our payment gateway (to process payment), our shipping carrier (to deliver), our email provider (to send receipts), and our hosting and database providers (to run the site). Each receives only the information reasonably necessary to perform its service and is expected to protect it appropriately. We may also disclose information where required by law, court order or a valid government request.

5. How long we keep it

Order and transaction records are ordinarily retained for five years from the date of purchase for accounting, compliance and product traceability. We may keep a record longer where required by law or reasonably necessary to resolve a dispute. Other data is deleted or anonymised when it is no longer needed for the purpose for which it was collected.

6. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, export or delete your personal data, and to object to or restrict its processing. To exercise any of these rights, email support@nuvamin.bio. We may need to verify your identity before completing a request. You may also lodge a complaint with the Ohio Attorney General or the privacy or data-protection authority responsible for your place of residence.

7. Cookies & local storage

The site uses browser local storage to remember your cart and your completed researcher-verification acknowledgement. We do not currently set advertising or cross-site tracking cookies. If our use of cookies or analytics changes, this policy will be updated before those tools are used where prior notice or consent is required.

8. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical and organisational safeguards designed to protect personal data from unauthorised access, loss, misuse or alteration. No internet transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

9. International processing

Your information may be processed in the United States and in other countries where our service providers operate. Where applicable law requires it, we use appropriate contractual or organisational safeguards for international transfers.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes to our services, providers or legal obligations. The current version will remain available on this page, and the date above identifies the month of the latest published version.
